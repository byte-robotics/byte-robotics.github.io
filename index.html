Biography

I am a 4th-year Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science and Technology in the Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Fuchun Sun. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Shandong University in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Gongping Yang.

My general research interests include computer vision, machine learning and robotics. My goal is to tell the machine what the world is like by computer vision technology.

More specifically, my recent research interests include general object detection, vision guided robotics grasping and 3D object matching.
