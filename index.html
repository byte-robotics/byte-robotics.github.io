Biography

 

 

I’m a 2nd-year Ph.D. candidate of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Tsinghua University  under the supervision of Prof. Fuchun Sun. Before that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology in Shandong University in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Gongping Yang. My recent research interests include computer vision, machine learning, especially, object detection and localization of general object.
