Intro

  • I am a Senior Researcher at ByteDance AI Lab.
  • At ByteDance, I lead research programs on developing next-generation robot techniques and systems. My research mission is to develop robot techniques and systems to perform intelligent perception, action, and interaction in real-world.I received my Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University, advised by Fuchun Sun. During my Ph.D. study, I visited the University of Pennsylvania, working with Jianbo Shi. I also interned at Intel Labs, Microsoft Research Asia and ByteDance. My thesis was nominated as CAAI Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award 2020.
  • We are recruiting interns / full-time researchers and developers in robotics. If you are interested in these positions, please drop me an email.
